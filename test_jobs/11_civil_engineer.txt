Civil Engineer I — Transportation & Infrastructure
Redstone Engineering Group | Seattle, WA | Full-Time

About Redstone
Redstone is a 180-person civil engineering and infrastructure consulting firm with a 30-year track record in transportation, water resources, and site development. We've delivered $800M in infrastructure projects across the Pacific Northwest, including highway interchanges, transit facilities, and stormwater systems.

The Role
We're looking for a Civil Engineer I to join our Transportation group, working on roadway design, intersection improvements, and multimodal corridor projects for WSDOT, Sound Transit, and local municipalities.

What You'll Do
- Perform roadway geometric design: horizontal and vertical alignment, cross-section development, sight distance analysis using AutoCAD Civil 3D
- Prepare and review engineering drawings, specifications, and construction documents for transportation improvement projects
- Conduct traffic and drainage calculations; support hydrological and hydraulic analyses using HEC-HMS/RAS or similar
- Prepare technical reports, quantity takeoffs, and cost estimates; support permitting application packages
- Attend design coordination meetings with clients, subconsultants, and reviewing agencies
- Support construction administration: respond to contractor RFIs, review submittals, conduct site visits

Required:
- Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from an ABET-accredited program
- EIT/FE exam passed (PE license pursuit expected)
- Proficiency in AutoCAD; AutoCAD Civil 3D experience preferred
- Understanding of roadway design standards (AASHTO, WSDOT Design Manual, ADA requirements)
- Strong technical writing skills; ability to prepare clear engineering reports

Preferred:
- Internship experience in transportation, site development, or civil engineering consulting
- Familiarity with hydrological/hydraulic software (HEC-RAS, StormCAD, SWMM)
- Exposure to traffic engineering software (Synchro, VISSIM) or GIS tools (ArcGIS, QGIS)
- Knowledge of NEPA environmental review process or permitting with regulatory agencies

Compensation: $68,000–$82,000 depending on experience. PE exam reimbursement, professional development support, hybrid schedule after 6-month onboarding period.
