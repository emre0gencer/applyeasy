Healthcare Analyst — Payer & Provider Strategy
Vela Health Advisors | Washington, DC (Hybrid) | Full-Time

About Vela
Vela Health Advisors is a boutique healthcare consulting firm advising payers, provider systems, and health tech companies on strategy, operations, and value-based care transformation. We're 65 people, growing steadily, and deeply focused on doing work that matters in healthcare — not just billing hours.

The Role
We're hiring a Healthcare Analyst to support consulting engagements focused on payer-provider contracting strategy, VBC program design, and healthcare market analysis. Analysts work directly with project managers and principals on small, focused teams.

What You'll Do
- Conduct quantitative and qualitative analysis to support strategy recommendations for health system and payer clients
- Build financial and operational models: total cost of care analysis, network performance benchmarking, ACO/VBC payment modeling
- Perform primary and secondary market research: payer landscape analysis, competitive intelligence, literature reviews on clinical program effectiveness
- Contribute to deliverables including strategic roadmaps, board presentations, and operational playbooks
- Interview client stakeholders (clinical, operational, financial leadership); synthesize findings into structured insights
- Support proposal development and client relationship activities

Required:
- Bachelor's degree in Public Health, Health Policy, Economics, Biology, or a quantitative field; Master's degree a plus
- Strong quantitative analysis skills: Excel modeling, data analysis, basic statistics
- Genuine interest in healthcare; ability to quickly develop working knowledge of payer/provider economics, healthcare policy, and clinical operations
- Clear, concise written communication; ability to produce polished client-ready materials
- Organized; able to manage multiple deliverables across concurrent engagements

Preferred:
- Internship experience in healthcare consulting, health system strategy, payer operations, or health policy
- Familiarity with value-based care models (ACOs, MSSP, PCMH, bundled payments)
- Exposure to healthcare claims data analysis or HEDIS quality metrics
- Python, R, or SQL skills for data analysis
- MPH, MHA, or MBA in progress or completed

Compensation: $70,000–$85,000 depending on degree and experience. Annual merit increase, 401(k), health benefits, and conference/training budget.
