Growth Marketing Associate
Velour — DTC Apparel Brand | Los Angeles, CA | Full-Time (Hybrid)

About Velour
Velour is a 4-year-old direct-to-consumer women's activewear brand doing $18M in annual revenue, growing 60% year-over-year. We sell primarily through our own website and through a tight community of brand advocates and micro-influencers. We don't do wholesale. Our team of 22 moves fast, tests constantly, and has strong opinions about why most marketing is bad.

The Role
We're hiring a Growth Marketing Associate to own our paid social and influencer channels. You'll manage real budget ($25K–$50K/month across Meta and TikTok), test creative relentlessly, and build the influencer program from a spreadsheet into a repeatable system. This role reports directly to the Head of Growth and has genuine ownership — you won't be scheduling posts for someone else's strategy.

What You'll Do
- Own day-to-day management of Meta Ads and TikTok Ads campaigns: campaign structure, audience targeting, bid strategy, budget pacing
- Develop and test creative briefs; coordinate with UGC creators and internal designers to produce 15–25 new ad variations per month
- Analyze campaign performance weekly; make data-driven optimization decisions on creative, targeting, and spend allocation to hit ROAS and CPA targets
- Build and manage a roster of 20–40 micro and mid-tier influencers: sourcing, outreach, contracting, briefing, and tracking affiliate link / promo code performance
- Set up and interpret A/B tests on ad copy, landing page variants, and email opt-in flows
- Maintain performance reporting dashboards in Google Data Studio (or similar); present weekly results to Head of Growth
- Identify and test emerging platforms and ad formats (TikTok Shop, Reels ads, etc.)

What We're Looking For
Required:
- 1–2 years of hands-on paid social experience, either at a DTC brand or a performance marketing agency; internship counts if the experience is substantive
- Direct experience managing Meta Ads campaigns (Ads Manager, not just boosted posts); basic understanding of campaign structure, pixel events, and attribution windows
- Comfortable with data: able to pull numbers, spot trends, and tell a coherent story about performance without being hand-held
- Strong written communication — you'll write ad copy, influencer briefs, and internal recommendations frequently
- Organized and self-directed; comfortable in a small team where "not my job" doesn't exist

Preferred:
- Experience managing TikTok Ads campaigns
- Hands-on influencer program management (outreach, contracting, tracking)
- Familiarity with Klaviyo or similar email/SMS platform
- Understanding of DTC e-commerce metrics: CAC, LTV, payback period, MER vs. ROAS
- Experience in apparel, beauty, wellness, or other lifestyle consumer categories

What We Don't Want
- Someone who needs an existing playbook handed to them
- Someone who uses "we saw good engagement" as a conclusion instead of a number

Compensation
- $55,000–$70,000 base salary depending on experience
- Performance bonus tied to channel ROAS targets
- Employee product discount (generous)
- Health benefits, flexible PTO
- Hybrid: 3 days/week in our Culver City office

To Apply: Resume + a 3–5 sentence description of a campaign or test you ran, what the result was, and what you'd do differently. No cover letter needed.
