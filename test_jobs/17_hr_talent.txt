Talent Acquisition Coordinator / Early-Career Recruiter
Stellarworks | Denver, CO (Hybrid) | Full-Time

About Stellarworks
Stellarworks is a 900-person professional services company providing technology staffing and managed services to healthcare and government clients. We're growing our internal Talent Acquisition team to support a hiring target of 400+ technical and professional roles per year.

The Role
We're looking for a Talent Acquisition Coordinator or junior Recruiter to join our TA team of 8. You'll own the full-cycle coordination for early-career and volume hiring pipelines, and grow toward owning requisitions independently within your first 6 months.

What You'll Do
- Source candidates through LinkedIn Recruiter, job boards, and employee referrals for technical and professional roles across healthcare IT and government services
- Screen resumes and conduct initial phone screens to assess experience, motivation, and compensation alignment
- Manage interview scheduling coordination across hiring managers, candidates, and panel members; maintain clean ATS records (Greenhouse)
- Extend offers, manage background check workflows, and shepherd candidates through onboarding hand-off
- Build and maintain talent pipelines for recurring role types (helpdesk, project coordinators, clinical analysts)
- Track and report weekly TA metrics: time-to-fill, source mix, offer acceptance rate, pipeline conversion
- Support employer branding initiatives: university outreach, career fair coordination, LinkedIn content

Required:
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business, Psychology, Communications, or a related field
- Strong organizational skills; comfortable managing 20–30 active candidate pipelines simultaneously
- Excellent interpersonal communication — you represent the company to every candidate you contact
- Proactive and self-directed; able to prioritize workload in a fast-moving environment
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office/Google Workspace; experience with any ATS is a plus

Preferred:
- Internship or part-time experience in HR, recruiting, talent acquisition, or staffing
- Familiarity with LinkedIn Recruiter or Boolean search techniques
- Exposure to ATS platforms (Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, iCIMS)
- Interest in growing toward full-cycle recruiting ownership
- Experience in technology staffing or professional services a plus

Compensation: $48,000–$60,000 base + performance bonus tied to hiring goals. Comprehensive benefits, 401(k), professional development stipend.
