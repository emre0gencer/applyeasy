Backend Software Engineer — New Grad / Early Career
Meridian Financial Technology | Boston, MA (Hybrid) | Full-Time

About Meridian
Meridian is a Series B fintech company building real-time payment infrastructure for mid-market banks and credit unions. We process $2B+ in transactions monthly across 140 financial institutions. Our engineering team of 35 moves fast, ships weekly, and owns what it builds end to end.

The Role
We're hiring a backend engineer to join our Core Payments team. You'll build and maintain the services that move money reliably at scale — think idempotent API design, distributed transaction management, and observability that actually tells you what's wrong. This is not a front-end or full-stack role; we want someone who wants to go deep on backend systems.

What You'll Do
- Design, build, and maintain REST and async APIs in Python (FastAPI) powering payment initiation, settlement, and reconciliation workflows
- Work with PostgreSQL and Redis for transactional data storage and caching; optimize queries and design schemas for high write throughput
- Build and monitor background job pipelines using Celery and RabbitMQ for settlement batch processing
- Write comprehensive test suites (unit, integration, contract) targeting 85%+ coverage across all owned services
- Participate in on-call rotation; respond to and post-mortem production incidents
- Collaborate with product and compliance to implement payment network requirements (ACH, RTP, Fedwire basics)
- Contribute to internal tooling and developer experience improvements

What We're Looking For
Required:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related technical field (or equivalent practical experience)
- Proficiency in Python; experience with FastAPI or a comparable async framework
- Understanding of relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred); ability to write performant SQL queries
- Familiarity with version control (Git), CI/CD pipelines, and code review practices
- Ability to write clean, well-tested code and document your work clearly

Preferred / Nice to Have:
- Experience with containerization (Docker) and deployment on cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Exposure to message queues or event-driven architectures (RabbitMQ, Kafka, SQS)
- Familiarity with observability tools (Datadog, Grafana, PagerDuty)
- Experience building or consuming webhook delivery systems
- Any internship or project experience in fintech, payments, or financial data

- Equity participation
