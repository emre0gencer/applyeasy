Content Strategist / Associate Content Manager
Fieldwork Media | Remote (US) | Full-Time

About Fieldwork
Fieldwork is a B2B content studio producing long-form editorial, research reports, and content programs for technology and professional services companies. Our clients include SaaS companies, consulting firms, and financial data providers. We believe good B2B content sounds like it was written by a smart person, not a content marketing department.

The Role
We're looking for an Associate Content Strategist who can research, structure, and write substantive B2B content — not someone who repurposes press releases or writes listicles. This role involves understanding complex subject matter, interviewing SMEs, and producing content that practitioners actually find valuable.

What You'll Do
- Research and write long-form content: industry reports (2,000–5,000 words), case studies, white papers, and executive-facing guides for technology and professional services clients
- Develop content strategy frameworks for clients: audience definition, topic clustering, distribution channel planning, editorial calendar development
- Conduct primary research: SME interviews (typically 3–5 per project), survey design and analysis, synthesis of third-party data sources
- Write and edit at a professional standard; produce drafts that require light editing (not heavy rewriting) from senior staff
- Collaborate with clients on topic approval, outline review, and revision cycles; manage project timelines and communication professionally
- Contribute to SEO strategy: keyword research, content gap analysis, on-page optimization recommendations

Required:
- Bachelor's degree in English, Journalism, Communications, Marketing, or a related field
- Strong writing ability — clear, concise, appropriately formal for B2B audiences, free of filler and vague language
- Genuine intellectual curiosity about business and technology topics; ability to quickly develop working knowledge in new domains
- Comfort with primary research: able to prepare for and conduct a professional interview with a senior executive or technical expert
- Organized and reliable; content projects live and die by deadline and communication

Preferred:
- Internship or freelance experience in content writing, journalism, or communications for a B2B or technology context
- Familiarity with SEO fundamentals: keyword intent, content structure for search, basic understanding of on-page factors
- Experience using SEO tools (Ahrefs, Semrush, or similar) for content research
- Exposure to content strategy concepts: buyer journey mapping, audience personas, content audits
- Writing samples in a B2B or technical context strongly preferred over consumer or social content

Compensation: $55,000–$70,000 base depending on experience. Fully remote, flexible hours, professional development budget ($1,200/year).
