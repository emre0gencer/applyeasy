Audit Associate — Public Accounting (Financial Services)
Barfield & Kessler LLP | New York, NY | Full-Time

About Barfield & Kessler
B&K is a top-25 public accounting firm with 1,200 professionals across 14 offices. Our Financial Services practice serves asset managers, private equity funds, hedge funds, and REITs with audit, tax, and advisory services. We're known for technical depth, training quality, and a culture that takes professional development seriously.

The Role
Audit Associates join client engagement teams and develop into well-rounded public accountants over a structured 2–3 year program. You'll work on financial statement audits for funds ranging from $100M to $10B AUM, gaining exposure to investment valuations, complex financial instruments, and the operational mechanics of institutional asset management.

What You'll Do
- Execute audit procedures for financial statements of investment funds, including cash, investments, income/expense, and equity accounts
- Prepare and review workpapers documenting audit evidence, controls testing, and conclusions in compliance with PCAOB/GAAS standards
- Analyze investment portfolios: verify fair value measurements, review broker confirmations, test NAV calculations
- Draft sections of audit reports, management representation letters, and other client deliverables
- Identify and communicate audit findings, control deficiencies, and accounting issues to senior associates and managers
- Build working relationships with client finance teams; gather information and resolve open items professionally

Required:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Accounting; 150 credit hours for CPA eligibility (or in progress)
- Strong understanding of GAAP and fundamental financial statement accounting
- CPA exam eligibility upon hire; sitting for first section within 6 months strongly preferred
- High attention to detail; ability to work accurately under deadline pressure
- Strong Excel skills; experience with audit software (ProSystem fx, CaseWare, or similar) a plus

Preferred:
- Public accounting internship (Big 4, national, or regional firm)
- Coursework or exposure to investment fund accounting, partnership taxation, or financial instruments
- Experience working in a team environment with multiple simultaneous project deadlines

Compensation: $72,000–$82,000 base + performance bonus. CPA exam fees and study materials covered. Comprehensive benefits.
