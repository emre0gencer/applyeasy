Investment Banking Analyst — Technology, Media & Telecom (TMT)
Caldwell & Wren | New York, NY | Full-Time

About Caldwell & Wren
Caldwell & Wren is a mid-market investment bank advising on M&A, debt and equity raises, and restructuring for growth-stage and established companies. Our TMT group focuses on transactions in the $50M–$500M range where we can add genuine strategic value — not just execute process. The group closed 18 transactions in 2023 totaling $2.4B in deal value.

The Role
We are looking for an Analyst to join the TMT investment banking team. Analysts at C&W are full participants in live transactions from day one — you will own financial models, help run diligence, build client materials, and interface directly with company management teams. This is a two-year program designed to develop serious deal professionals.

What You'll Do
- Build and maintain complex financial models: 3-statement models, DCF valuations, accretion/dilution analysis, and LBO models for sell-side, buy-side, and financing transactions
- Prepare pitch books, confidential information memoranda (CIMs), management presentations, and board materials
- Conduct industry research and comparable company / precedent transaction analysis using Bloomberg, CapIQ, and PitchBook
- Support due diligence processes: review data room documents, manage information requests to counterparties, flag issues to senior bankers
- Communicate directly with clients and counterparties via email and occasional calls under senior banker supervision
- Manage multiple simultaneous workstreams across 2–4 live transactions

What We're Looking For
Required:
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a quantitative field from an accredited institution; strong GPA preferred
- Demonstrated financial modeling skills: 3-statement, DCF, and comparable company analysis (tested in process)
- Proficiency in Excel and PowerPoint; Bloomberg Terminal and CapIQ experience a strong plus
- Understanding of accounting fundamentals: income statement, balance sheet, cash flow statement, GAAP principles
- High attention to detail; able to produce error-free client-facing materials under deadline pressure
- Strong written and verbal communication skills; professional presence in client settings

Preferred:
- Prior investment banking, equity research, or private equity internship experience
- Exposure to M&A transaction processes (sell-side or buy-side)
- CFA Level I passed or registered
- Knowledge of TMT sector dynamics and business models (SaaS metrics, media rights, telco capex structures)
- VBA / Python skills for automation of repetitive modeling tasks

This Is NOT a Fit If You:
- Are looking for a 40-hour-week role (this is a demanding 2-year program)
- Have not worked through at least one live transaction or substantial internship modeling experience

Compensation
- $110,000 base salary + year-end bonus (target 50–100% of base depending on deal activity and performance)
- Benefits: medical, dental, vision, 401(k) match
- Structured training program in first 90 days; ongoing mentorship from VP and MD level

Application: Resume + cover letter required. Modeling test administered in second-round interview.
